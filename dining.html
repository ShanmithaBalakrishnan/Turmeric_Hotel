Indulge in the Best of Both Worlds

What cuisines are offered at Simmer Sizzle?

Simmer Sizzle features a diverse range of international cuisines, including Asian, European, and Fusion dishes.

Is there a buffet option available?

Yes, Simmer Sizzle offers an extensive buffet spread during breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

What are the restaurant’s operating hours?

Breakfast: 07:30 AM – 10:00 AM
Lunch: 12:30 PM – 03:00 PM
Dinner: 07:30 PM – 10:00 PM

What type of cuisine does Thenmanam serve?

Thenmanam specializes in traditional South Indian cuisine, with a focus on Kongu region and tribal flavors.

Are there vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available?

Yes, Thenmanam offers a variety of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
